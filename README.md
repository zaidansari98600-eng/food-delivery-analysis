# 🍔 Food Delivery Operations Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
SQL-based analysis of 2,784 food delivery orders across 8 Indian cities to identify actionable business insights on ordering patterns, delivery performance, and customer preferences.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **MySQL** — Data querying and analysis
- **Tableau** — Interactive dashboard and visualizations

## 📊 Key Insights
- Jaipur has the highest average order value at ₹1,003
- Fast Food is the most ordered cuisine with 1,463 total orders
- Delhi has the fastest average delivery time at 47.1 minutes
- Peak ordering hours are 12 PM (Lunch) and 7 PM (Dinner)
- Subway is the highest rated restaurant with an average rating of 4.15

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- **Target premium partnerships in Jaipur** — Highest avg order value (₹1,003) indicates strong spending power; ideal city for premium restaurant onboarding
- **Optimize delivery staffing for peak hours** — 12 PM and 7 PM see the highest order volumes; allocating more delivery agents during these windows can reduce delays and improve customer satisfaction
- **Use Delhi as a delivery benchmark** — At 47.1 mins average, Delhi outperforms other cities; study its operational model and replicate in slower cities
- **Double down on Fast Food inventory & partnerships** — With 1,463 orders, Fast Food dominates demand; ensuring restaurant availability and faster prep times in this category will directly boost revenue
- **Leverage Subway's rating for marketing** — A 4.15 avg rating with high order volume makes Subway an ideal brand to feature in promotions and campaigns

## 🔍 SQL Queries Covered
1. City-wise average order value
2. Cuisine-wise order volume and revenue
3. Average delivery time by city
4. Peak ordering hours analysis
5. Top 10 highest rated restaurants
